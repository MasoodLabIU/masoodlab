Masood Lab

At Masood Lab, our mission is to unravel the intricate world of tumors and their microenvironments at the single-cell level. We employ cutting-edge single-cell and spatial techniques, supported by advanced computational analysis, to uncover novel biomarkers and potent therapeutic solutions.

We are committed to delving into the complexities of intra-tumoral heterogeneity, understanding the relationships within tumor ecosystems, investigating patient outcomes, and overcoming treatment resistance. Our multidisciplinary team of clinical scientists, pathologists, and computational biologists collaborates to enhance clinical outcomes for individuals battling gastrointestinal cancer, dedicated to pioneering transformative research.
