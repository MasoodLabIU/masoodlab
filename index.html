Masood Lab

At the Masood Lab, we are dedicated to transforming our understanding of cancer biology through the lens of translational oncology and advanced computational science. Our research program centers on deconstructing the tumor microenvironment (TME) at single-cell resolution, investigating how complex cellular interactions dictate disease progression, therapeutic resistance, and clinical outcomes.

By integrating high-dimensional spatial transcriptomics, single-cell multi-omics, and custom bioinformatics pipelines, we aim to map the architecture of the tumor ecosystem. Our mission is to bridge the gap between bench-side discovery and clinical application, identifying novel actionable targets that empower the next generation of precision immunotherapy and symptom management strategies. We are committed to fostering an evidence-based approach that turns complex data into meaningful improvements in patient care and medical policy.
